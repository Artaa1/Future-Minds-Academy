Climate in Prizren

Prizren has an oceanic climate (Köppen climate classification Cfa) with an average annual temperature of 10.7 °C (51.3 °F).
The warmest month in Prizren is August with an average temperature of 21.9 °C (71.4 °F), while the coldest month is January with an average temperature of −0.5 °C (31.1 °F).
