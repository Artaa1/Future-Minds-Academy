POWDERED MILK

Mother's milk , the most natural source of nourishment for the baby. It is not possible to find a food that can take 100 percent the place of mother's milk.

But in the absence of mother's breast milk, it is replaced with powdered milk.

Even powdered milk is the best substitute for breast milk.
