Heat transfer, in the plate heat exchanger - Project 1

This project was conducted using the COMSOL program, which, through calculations, demonstrated changes in temperature, pressure, and the velocity of the cooler.
The plate heat exchanger (PFHE) employs finned plates to efficiently transfer heat. These fins serve to enhance the structural integrity of the plates, allowing them to withstand high pressures and providing an extended heat transfer area.
The materials used during the development of this model are:

  • Silica Glass
  • Air
  • Aluminum 3003-H183



Below are presented, the change of temperature, pressure and speed of the cooler



Temperature

In the temperature graph which is updated after the calculation, we can notice that the presence of the thin layer of air between the chip and the heatsink induced about 10°C increase in the maximum temperature.


Pressure

The increase in pressure drop causes an increase in the average velocity, which increases the volumetric flow, and this volumetric increase helps remove heat.


Cooling speed

As the cooler speed increases, the cooler will cool the fins more efficiently.

Turbine and types of turbines - Project 2

Steam Turbines

A turbine is a rotating heat engine that converts the thermal energy of a working body in mechanical work.
The turbine consists of two main:

  • Rotating part - the rotor
  • Mounted part - frame (stator)

The figure below shows the steam turbine

The types of turbines are:

  • Active turbine
  • Reactive turbine
